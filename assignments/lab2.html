Lab 2: Password Cracking

This lab is worth 3 points + potential bonus.

Download the following files copied from a Linux server on the Tufts campus:

  1. passwd
  2. shadow

Using a password cracker of your choice (John the Ripper anyone?), crack as many of the passwords that you can and answer the questions at https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1XcWSYk8C7cUOvHRrMwp6NU-SKTCbqL62Hmhk3_3ZyTM/viewform

Please be sure to keep records of your cracked passwords, including screenshots and logs.
